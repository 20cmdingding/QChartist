You can open End Of Day charts :
- stock charts with Yahoo Finance (e.g. symbol : yhoo)
- forex charts with Stooq (e.g. symbol : eurusd)

Notice : if you choose the monthly or weekly timeframe and/or a young symbol, be sure that you put a small number for "displayed bars" (10 for exemple) else it may freeze. After the chart is displayed, you can increase the displayed bars number.
